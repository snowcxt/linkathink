Find Your Flow

Dive into a tranquil puzzle experience where every drop matters. Cultivate vibrant schools of fish in a watery grid, but remember—every connected school must always touch at least two sources of fresh "Live Water" to keep the ecosystem in balance.

Plan carefully. Preserve water. Create harmony. A meditative challenge that starts simple and grows into deep, satisfying strategy.

With hundreds of handcrafted levels, calming visuals, and a relaxing soundtrack, Live Water is your escape to a world of peaceful problem-solving.

Master the Elements

Start with the core puzzle, then expand your strategy with new mechanics

Live Water

Preserve empty cells as the lifeline for your schools. Every fish group needs at least two adjacent Live Water cells to thrive.

Locked Fish

Build your strategy around fixed starting points (marked with a dashed circle). They cannot be moved.

Walls

Block water flow and fish placement. Plan your paths carefully around these obstacles.

Dangerous Water

Provides precious water flow, but no fish can survive there. Use it wisely to supply your schools.

Lotuses

Connect these numbered plants to fill the board—they thrive without needing live water nearby.
